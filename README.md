# Overview

HumanizeCity is an open-source initiative that bridges architecture, urban planning, geographic information systems (GIS), data science, and artificial intelligence to support evidence-based urban decision-making.

Rather than being a single application, HumanizeCity is designed as a growing ecosystem of interoperable tools, research methodologies, datasets, analytical frameworks, and software components that help researchers, practitioners, public agencies, and educators better understand, analyze, and improve cities.

---

# Why HumanizeCity?

Cities generate enormous volumes of spatial, environmental, mobility, behavioral, and socioeconomic data.

Meanwhile, decades of valuable urban research remain scattered across journal articles, reports, isolated software, and disconnected datasets.

HumanizeCity aims to bridge this gap by transforming research outputs into reusable open-source digital tools that make urban knowledge more transparent, accessible, reproducible, and actionable.

---

# From Research to Practice

One of HumanizeCity's fundamental principles is that academic research should not end with publication.

Instead, research should evolve into practical software that supports better planning, better policies, and better cities.

HumanizeCity transforms:

- Research methodologies
- Urban indicators
- Design standards
- Planning frameworks
- Analytical models
- Decision-support methods

into reusable open-source software modules that anyone can improve and extend.
