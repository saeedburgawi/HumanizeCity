# Introduction

## What is HumanizeCity?

HumanizeCity is an open-source research platform that supports human-centered urban analysis by combining architecture, urban planning, geographic information systems (GIS), data science, and artificial intelligence.

The project provides an open foundation for developing tools that help researchers, practitioners, and public organizations better understand, analyze, and improve the built environment.

---

## Why HumanizeCity?

Cities are becoming increasingly complex, yet many urban research workflows remain fragmented across different software, datasets, and disciplines.

HumanizeCity aims to bring these components together within an open, extensible, and collaborative ecosystem.

---

## Objectives

The project has several core objectives:

- Support evidence-based urban decision-making.
- Promote reproducible research.
- Develop reusable open-source tools.
- Encourage interdisciplinary collaboration.
- Improve accessibility to urban analytics.

---

## Who is HumanizeCity for?

HumanizeCity is designed for:

- Researchers
- Urban planners
- Architects
- Housing specialists
- GIS professionals
- Data scientists
- Universities
- Public agencies
- Students

---

## Current Status

HumanizeCity is currently under active development.

The current release establishes the project's structure, documentation, and development workflow.

Future releases will introduce analytical modules, GIS capabilities, AI-assisted tools, datasets, and interactive dashboards.
