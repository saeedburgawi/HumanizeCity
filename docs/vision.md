# Vision

## Our Vision

HumanizeCity aims to become a leading open-source ecosystem for human-centered urban analytics, enabling researchers, planners, architects, and public agencies to make evidence-based decisions that improve cities and communities.

Rather than focusing solely on software, HumanizeCity seeks to bridge architecture, urban planning, geographic information systems (GIS), artificial intelligence, and open science into a single collaborative platform.

---

## Mission

Our mission is to develop transparent, reproducible, and accessible open-source tools that support:

- Urban analytics
- Housing research
- Human-centered design
- Spatial analysis
- AI-assisted planning
- Smart city research

---

## Guiding Principles

### Human-Centered

Technology should serve people and improve quality of life.

### Open Science

Research methods, data, and software should be transparent whenever possible.

### Evidence-Based Decision Making

Urban decisions should rely on measurable evidence rather than assumptions.

### Reproducibility

Research workflows should be reproducible and reusable by other researchers.

### Interdisciplinary Collaboration

Architecture, planning, engineering, computer science, and data science should work together.

---

## Long-Term Goals

HumanizeCity aims to become:

- an open-source urban analytics platform
- a research infrastructure for architecture and housing
- a repository of reusable urban datasets
- a collaborative environment for researchers
- an educational resource for universities
- a foundation for AI-assisted urban planning

## Looking Ahead

HumanizeCity is envisioned as a long-term open-source initiative that grows through collaboration, research, and continuous innovation.

We welcome ideas, contributions, and partnerships from individuals and organizations who share the goal of building more livable, sustainable, and human-centered cities.

## HumanizeCity Ecosystem

HumanizeCity is envisioned as an ecosystem of interoperable open-source projects rather than a single application.

Future components may include:

- HumanizeCity Core
- HumanizeCity Analytics
- HumanizeCity GIS
- HumanizeCity AI
- HumanizeCity Datasets
- HumanizeCity Research
- HumanizeCity Education
